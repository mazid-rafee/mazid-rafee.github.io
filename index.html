Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate at Florida International University, conducting research on environmental data analytics, machine learning, and water quality forecasting at HPDRC under Dr. Naphtali Rishe.

My research focuses on developing deep learning frameworks for environmental sensing and irregular time-series forecasting. I integrate LSTM/BiLSTM and ODE models for spatio-temporal prediction. I am also experienced in data engineering, database systems, and large-scale data pipelines.

Previously, I worked as a Senior Software Engineer at Enosis Solutions, leading backend development for large SaaS healthcare applications.
